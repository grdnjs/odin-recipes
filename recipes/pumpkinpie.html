Pumpkin Pie

Home

Description

This classic pumpkin pie features a rich, silky filling with warm spices in a flaky homemade crust. Perfect for your holiday table, this traditional dessert balances sweet pumpkin flavor with aromatic spices for a comforting end to any meal.

Ingredients

  • One 9" pie crust
  • One 16oz can of pumpkin puree
  • One 14oz can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F (220C).
  2. Put pie crust in pie dish and then on baking sheet.
  3. Combine puree, condensed milk, eggs, and spice into large bowl and mix.
  4. Pour mixture into crust. Bake for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350F (175C) and bake 35-40 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and set on wire rack to cool.
  6. Serve ang enjoy!
