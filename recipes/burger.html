Description

This juicy, all-American favorite features a perfectly seasoned beef patty grilled to perfection, nestled between two soft, toasted buns. Topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, and our special sauce, each bite delivers a harmonious blend of savory flavors and satisfying textures. Whether you're craving comfort food or celebrating a summer cookout, our classic burger promises to satisfy with its timeless appeal and mouthwatering taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 0.5c dry bread crumbs, fine
  1. Preheat outdoor grill on high heat.
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and breadcrumbs. Mix until well blended.
  4. Form into 0.75" thick patties.
  5. Grill 6-8 minutes per side, to desired doneness. Internal temperature should read at least 160F (70C).
  6. Put between buns with desired toppings. Serve hot.
