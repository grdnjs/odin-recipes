Description

This soul-warming Japanese staple features tender, springy noodles swimming in a rich, complex broth that's been simmered for hours to develop deep umami flavors. Topped with slices of succulent chashu pork, a perfectly soft-boiled egg, fresh scallions, and crisp nori, each bowl offers a harmonious balance of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • 3.5c vegetable broth
  • 3.5oz package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 0.5 tsp chili oil
  • 0.5 tsp minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. Combine broth and noodles in medium saucepan. Cover and bring to boil on high heat, stirring.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in sesame oil.
  4. Garnish with onions and serve.
